After a successful event last year, the Woodhouse Eaves Horticultural and Craft Show is happening again in 2026. Everyone, young and old, are welcome to enter and take part.

Entry forms will be available from all local shops from August onwards, or you can download and print the entry form as an A4 page.

The show will be open for visitors from 2pm onwards with a £1 entry fee for adults and free for children.

This is a cash only event.
