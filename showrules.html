Show Rules

  • All classes are open to both female and male exhibitors.
  • Exhibits MUST be the product of the house/garden/allotment of the exhibitor.
  • Staging of exhibits may start at 8.30am and MUST BE COMPLETED by 10.45am
  • There is an entry fee of 50p per exhibit for adults. Children's entries are free, unless entering adult classes.
  • Exhibitors to provide their own containers and plates.
  • Exhibitors' adhesive numbers should be clearly visible, particularly in the culinary classes. They should NOT be fixed to the cling film, but on the TOP edge of the plate or dish.
  • For all cut flower exhibits bring your own vase. These need to be collected at the end of the show.
  • Village Hall exhibits SHALL NOT be removed before 4pm. Those remaining after 5pm will be deemed a gift and sold for Show funds. This includes vases from cut flower exhibits.
  • Any exhibitor wishing to lodge an objection may do so to any Committee Member before 3pm with a fee of £5, refunded if the complaint is upheld. The decision of the Committee or judge is final.
  • Points awarded 1st = 3pts, 2nd = 2pts, 3rd = 1pt
  • Judges may withhold any prize from any Class where they consider that there are insufficient entries or that the exhibits are of insufficient merit or quality to justify awards.
  • Garnishing of exhibits with parsley or foliage is allowed. A black base cloth is also permitted.
  • The Committee are not responsible for the loss or damage to any exhibit.
  • In the Flowers/Vegetable/Fruit Section only one entry in each Class is allowed from a family.
  • Exhibits that have previously won a prize in this Show cannot be re-entered. However, if you have previously entered an item in the Craft or Children's sections, and it did not win a prize, then it may be entered in the Show again.
  • The Allotment Prize-winner is not eligible to win again for the following year.
  • Viewing of the exhibits by the public is from 2pm to 4pm. Admission is £1 for adults and free for children.
  • Trophies to be held for one year.
  • Only one entry per person, per class, is permitted.
